Machine Lab

Official Hack The Box machines created by both moderators and community members.

What are VIP Machines?

VIP machines have been retired from our active machine lab and are no longer available on our free servers. We release one new machine every week, and also retire one machine at the same time.

When a machine is retired, its points are removed from all users. We publish a full walk-through for it and also allow members of the public to post their own solutions.

What is VIP 2.0?

When we first created VIP, our retired machine pool was much smaller. With a rotation of just 20 retired machines per week, and a total of 160 retired machines, it has become challenging for users to get the machine they want. As a result, we have created a new system which allows VIP users to spawn retired machines on demand.

Become VIP today and get access to our retired machine pool! Click here for more information.
